About The Show

"We don't do talking points. We do honest conversations — the kind that make you stop and think."

I'm On Earth Where Are You is a dynamic podcast dedicated to producing high-quality, engaging, and informative audio content for a diverse audience. Through compelling storytelling, insightful discussions, and candid interviews, we explore what it means to navigate life with your head on straight.

No agenda. No spin. Just common sense — and the occasional uncomfortable truth.
